# ORIGIN Item Rules

## Rule 1

One listing belongs to one owner.

---

## Rule 2

Every listing requires at least 3 photos.

---

## Rule 3

Every listing must belong to one category.

---

## Rule 4

Every listing has:

Rental Price

Security Deposit

Condition

Pickup Location

---

## Rule 5

A booked item cannot receive another booking for the same period.

---

## Rule 6

Deleted listings are archived.

Never permanently removed.

---

## Rule 7

Every listing must be approved before becoming public.
